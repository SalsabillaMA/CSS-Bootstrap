Card ukuran col-sm-6

The energy crisis triggered by the increase in world oil prices has also crushed the lives of various layers of society in Indonesia, because the level of demand for fuel oil continues to increase, therefore alternative energy is needed to replace BBM, so that the chain of life on this earth can run well. Many non-BBM alternative energy can be developed, one of which is biogas. The utilization of biogas energy sources has begun to be developed at the Swadaya Pandan Mulyo Agriculture and Rural Training Center but the production capacity of the existing system is still not optimal, this is because the existing equipment is still manual and simple so extra energy is needed in order to continue producing biogas.
